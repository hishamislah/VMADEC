About Us

VMADEC, originally known as Vazhemedathil Offset Printers, has been a trusted name in the printing industry for over five decades. Our journey began with a commitment to quality and customer satisfaction, and today, we continue to uphold these values while embracing the latest advancements in printing technology.

Our team of skilled professionals, state-of-the-art machinery, and eco-friendly practices enable us to deliver exceptional results for clients across various sectors, including healthcare, hospitality, education, and more. We believe in building lasting relationships with our clients by consistently exceeding their expectations and delivering on our promises.

Our Mission

To provide innovative, high-quality printing solutions that empower our clients to communicate their message effectively and leave a lasting impression.

Our Vision

To be the leading printing partner of choice, recognized for our integrity, reliability, and commitment to excellence in every project we undertake.
